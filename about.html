About Sprout & Sass

Sprout & Sass started with a simple belief: the people who name their houseplants, talk to their succulents, and panic over a drooping fern deserve apparel as charming as they are.

What we make

We design witty, botanical-inspired tees and gifts — equal parts cozy cottagecore and cheeky plant pun. From "Aloe You Vera Much" to "Resting Plant Face," every design is drawn to make a fellow plant lover smile.

How it works

Our products are printed on demand, which means each item is made fresh when you order it. That keeps waste low and quality high — no warehouses full of unsold shirts. Browse here, then check out securely through our Etsy shop, where payment, shipping, and buyer protection are all handled for you.

Say hello

We love hearing from fellow plant people. Whether it's a custom design idea, a wholesale question, or just to share a photo of your monstera, reach out anytime at momooffice1738@gmail.com.
