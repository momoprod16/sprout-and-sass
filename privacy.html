Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sprouts & Sass ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with this website (the "Site"). We are committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about our limited data practices.

1. Who we are

Sprouts & Sass is a small print-on-demand business offering botanical-inspired apparel and gifts. This Site is an informational storefront that showcases our designs and links to our shop on Etsy, where all sales are completed.

2. Information we collect

This Site is a static, informational website. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not operate checkout, payment, or order forms on this Site. As a result:

  • We do not collect names, addresses, payment details, or login credentials through this Site.
  • The Site does not require you to submit any personal information to browse it.
  • Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested) for security and reliability. We do not use this information to identify you.

3. Purchases are made on Etsy

When you choose to buy a product, you are directed to our shop on Etsy. Any information you provide to complete a purchase — including your name, shipping address, and payment details — is collected and processed by Etsy under Etsy's Privacy Policy, not by us. Our products are fulfilled through Printify, a print-on-demand provider; order and shipping details necessary to produce and deliver your item are shared between Etsy and Printify to fulfill your order.

4. Contacting us by email

If you email us, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry and do not sell or share it.

5. Cookies and analytics

This Site does not set advertising or tracking cookies of its own. If we add basic, privacy-respecting analytics in the future to understand general traffic, we will update this policy to describe it.

6. Third-party links

The Site contains links to third-party services such as Etsy and Pinterest. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's privacy

This Site is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your choices and rights

Because we do not collect personal information through this Site, there is generally no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. For information you provided to Etsy in connection with a purchase, please use the privacy controls described in Etsy's Privacy Policy. You may also contact us with any privacy question at the address below.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at momooffice1738@gmail.com.
